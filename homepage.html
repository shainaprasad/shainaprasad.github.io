I was born and raised by the Bay in the suburbs of San Francisco and currently reside near the waves in SF.

My curiosity of the human condition and its nuances drove me to study Sociology and User Experience, and eventually become a mixed-methods researcher. Over the last 6 years, this has led me through a variety of ventures from climate justice to tech to education, and taught me how these all intersect in the highly complex world we live in.

I have been an educator, writer, and program/project manager in the past, which shaped my scope of work and ability to empathize with unique groups. I am experienced with a range of clients, budgets, and goals.

I find excitement in solving complex problems with innovative approaches and enjoy thinking outside the box to make impactful change. My specialties lie in my ability to ensure inclusivity when selecting methods or target users.
